## WHAT THIS IS

Maille et Merveille is a brand of handmade story-driven dolls.

Each doll is a physical object and a digital character simultaneously.
When a child receives a doll, they do not just receive a toy.
They receive a companion with a name, a personality, a world, a voice, and a story.

The system that runs this business is called the Doll Lifecycle System.
It manages everything from the first creative idea through to a sold and archived doll.
It is the operational backbone of the entire business.

## THE BRAND

### Origin
Somewhere in a small house in the woods, handmade dolls quietly awaken —
waiting for the right child to bring them to life.

This is the brand's mythological origin. It is never shown directly.
It lives in the maker's voice, in the Instagram presence, in the space between
the physical doll and the world it carries.

### Voice
Soft. Warm. Motherly. Magical. Whispering to a child's imagination.

This is a storytelling voice, not a marketing voice.
It speaks the way a gentle adult reads a bedtime story.

### Emotional promise
To the child: "This feels like a real little best friend made just for me."
To the parent: "This is meaningful, beautiful, safe, and truly enriching."

### What Maille et Merveille never does
- Never sells. Always invites.
- Never loud, exaggerated, or overstimulating.
- Never treats the doll as a product. Always as a living character.
- Never introduces fear, unresolved conflict, or anxiety.
- Never writes down to children. Always speaks to their imagination.

## THE UNIVERSE SYSTEM

A universe is a fully rendered emotional world.
It defines the environment in which a collection of dolls and items live.
Every doll permanently belongs to one universe.

A universe provides:
- Visual identity: colors, backgrounds, animations, atmosphere
- Audio identity: ambient sounds, universe-level audio
- Narrative identity: a story library and activity library specific to that world
- Tonal identity: how content in this world feels and sounds

### The three active universes

**Farm World** — Warmth
Earthy, grounding, nurturing. The world of gentle mornings, animals, and
the quiet satisfaction of daily care. Content is close, intimate, already
in relationship with the child.

**Little Dreamers** — Grace and creativity
Ballerinas, artists, musicians. Delicate, poetic, expressive.
The world of imagination made visible. Content invites the child forward
into their own creative self.

**Together World** — Belonging and friendship
Nursery, school, playground. The world of first friendships,
finding your people, the child who saves you a seat.
Content is warm, social, and full of quiet joy.

### Universe rules
- The universe is created before any doll is made.
- Universes expand over time — new dolls and items are added to existing universes.
- Each doll belongs to one universe permanently.
- Cross-universe interactions are rare, special, and always intentional.
- Stories and activities belong to the universe, not to individual dolls.

## THE LIVING UNIVERSE MODEL

This is one of the most important principles in the entire system.

**The universe is the living entity. Not the doll.**

A sold doll is never updated directly. The universe she belongs to grows —
and she grows with it. Every child who owns a doll in a universe benefits
automatically from every new story, every new activity, every new character
that arrives in that universe. No effort per sold doll. No per-doll update
pipeline. The universe does the work.

### What this means for a child
When a child scans their doll's QR code, they do not enter a static page
about that doll. They enter the doll's living universe.

- New stories appear when the universe releases them
- New activities appear when the universe adds them
- New friends appear when new dolls join the universe
- Seasonal moments arrive when the universe celebrates them

The doll is the child's permanent passport into that universe.
She never expires. She never becomes outdated.
She is always the child's guide into a world that keeps getting richer.

### What this means commercially
We never market directly to a sold doll's owner.
We grow the universe — and the universe does the marketing.

When a new Farm World doll arrives, every child who owns any Farm World doll
discovers a new friend has arrived in their world. That is not a marketing
message. That is a story moment. The universe just grew.

When a new Farm World story is released, every Farm World doll page gains
a new story to explore. One creation reaches every child in that universe.

A parent whose child loves Farm World sees a new Farm World doll on Instagram.
Their child already has Rosie. Now there is a new friend for Rosie.
That is an upsell that feels like a gift, not a sale.

The narrative does the selling. We never break the magic.

### The content access model
Every story and activity has an access scope set by the operator at publish time.

| Scope | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Universe | Available to every doll in this universe automatically |
| Character match | Available to dolls whose character profile matches |
| Assigned | Operator manually assigns to specific dolls |
| Exclusive | Permanently locked to one specific doll only |

Default scope is Universe. The system resolves what content is available
for each doll at render time — no per-doll update needed for universe-wide content.

### New arrivals
When a new doll joins a universe, every existing doll's public page
reflects the new arrival in the universe discovery section.
A child scanning Rosie's QR discovers that Clara has just arrived
in Farm World — automatically, with no additional production effort.

## THE TWO OBJECT TYPES

### Dolls
A doll is the primary product and the primary character.

Every doll has:
- A physical form: crocheted by hand, one of a kind
- A character identity: name, personality, emotional core, signature trait
- A universe: the world she belongs to
- A voice: generated via ElevenLabs, consistent across all her content
- A story access: stories from the universe library (scoped per content access model)
- Activity access: universe activities, personalized with her name
- A digital experience: a public page accessed via QR code
- A production record: tracked from concept to sold

### Items
An item is an object related to a doll — a bag, a garment, an animal, a pet,
an accessory, or any other element that belongs in the doll's world.

Items are:
- Checked into the system like dolls
- Linked to a specific doll or universe
- Given digital production based on type:
  - Animals and creatures: may have a voice
  - Clothing and accessories: simpler record, no voice
- Featured in social content alongside their linked doll
- Sold solo or bundled with the doll

## THE THREE LAYERS OF OPERATION

### Layer 1 — Creative Lab
Where everything begins.

The creative director (currently the founder, AI-assisted) conceives:
- New universes
- Doll concepts with full character specs
- Item concepts
- Story templates and activity types

AI assists at every step — generating ideas, organizing thinking,
optimizing creative briefs. The creative director makes all decisions.

**Current mode:** The first batch of 50 dolls was created by an external
crochet artist and handed to the founder. Universes are being built
retrospectively around these dolls. Layer 1 is operational for universe
design but is being applied retroactively to the first batch.

**Future mode:** Doll concepts are fully defined in Layer 1 before any
physical production begins. Full specs — character, universe, physical
requirements — are handed to crochet artists as production briefs.

### Layer 2 — Physical Production
The journey from spec to finished physical doll.

Steps:
1. Specs handed off to crochet artist with expected delivery timeline
2. In production — status tracked in system
3. Reception — doll received, inspected, photographed
4. Quality check — pass leads to check-in, fail leads to reject or rework
5. Check-in — doll enters the digital production pipeline

**Current mode:** First batch is already finished. Physical production
tracking is not yet active in the system. Check-in is the current entry point.

**Future mode:** Full Layer 2 tracking is operational. Multiple crochet
artists work from system-generated specs. Quality gate enforced before
any doll enters digital production.

### Layer 3 — Digital Production
The journey from checked-in doll to live digital product.

Five stages:
1. **Check-in** — Name, character, picture, DNA, universe assignment captured
2. **Digital production** — Voice generated, story assigned, content created,
   QR activated, public page live
3. **Social media** — Content pushed to social media production pipeline,
   posts scheduled and published on Instagram
4. **Commerce** — Doll listed, priced, QR active, ready for sale
5. **Sold and archived** — Delivery tracked, record permanently frozen

A sold doll's record is frozen. Her universe continues to grow around her.

## THE CONTENT BUILDER

Stories and activities are created in the content builder.
This is a distinct system within the platform — a creative tool for operators.
All content is created at the universe level and flows down to dolls
through the content access model.

### Stories
A story is a narrative experience delivered through the public digital page.
Stories belong to a universe. Access scope determines which dolls receive them.

**Story types (planned):**
- Flat linear stories — the child follows a narrative
- Branched stories — the child makes choices that shape the story

**Story production lifecycle:**
Template selection → element assembly (narration, sounds, images, minimal text)
→ AI-assisted refinement → preview → approval → publish → scope assignment

**AI role in stories:**
The operator is the director. AI assists with pacing, narration style,
soundscape suggestions, copy refinement, and content optimization.
The operator steers every decision.

### Activities
An activity is an interactive experience the child engages with on the
public digital page. Activities belong to a universe.
Access scope determines which dolls receive them.
Doll name is personalized at render time.

**Activity types (starting set):**
- Tap and Discover — tap hidden elements to reveal sounds and animations
- Color and Decorate — digital colouring of universe scenes
- Story Choice — one question, three warm outcomes (no wrong answer)
- Do It With Your Doll — offline imaginative play prompted by the page

**Activity production lifecycle:**
Activity type selection → element assembly → scope assignment
→ preview → publish → available per scope

## THE PUBLIC DIGITAL EXPERIENCE

Every doll has a public page accessed by scanning their QR code.
This is the portal for everything the child experiences digitally.

The page serves two types of content simultaneously:
1. Doll-specific content — her name, voice, character, exclusive content
2. Universe content — stories, activities, new arrivals scoped to her universe

**Current scenes:**
- Welcome — the doll greets the child in her own voice
- Story — the doll's story, narrated page by page
- Play — an interactive story choice moment
- Meet Friends — universe discovery portal (new arrivals, stories, activities)

**Current capabilities:**
- Ambient animations (universe-aware particles, breathing, entrance effects)
- Voice narration (intro and story, page-level and continuous modes)
- Ambient universe audio layer
- Scene-level audio

**Planned additions:**
- Activities scene
- Expanded story types (branching)
- Seasonal and event-based content variants
- Animated short videos
- Universe discovery — new characters and content surfaced automatically

The public experience is fully separate from the admin and production system.
It never depends on pipeline state, readiness, or commerce logic at render time.

## SOCIAL MEDIA AND SALES

### The sales model
Instagram is the primary discovery and sales channel.
A parent discovers Maille et Merveille through an Instagram post.
They learn more through the doll's public page (QR or link).
They purchase through a direct conversation — DM, WhatsApp, or Telegram.

This is the current model. It is intentional for a premium handmade product
where every sale is personal and every doll is one of a kind.

**Future:** An online shop will be built. Direct conversation remains
as a customer service and relationship layer. The shop enables scale.

### The Instagram strategy principle
Instagram announces universe growth — not product sales.

When a new doll arrives in Farm World, Instagram tells the Farm World story.
When a new story drops, Instagram gives a glimpse of the world.
When a seasonal moment arrives, Instagram celebrates it.

Parents who follow Maille et Merveille are following a living universe.
The purchase impulse comes from belonging to that universe —
not from being sold to.

### Social media content strategy (to be developed)
The social media content strategy is not yet fully defined.
It will be built as a dedicated planning phase once the production system
is fully operational.

**What is known:**
- Instagram is the primary platform (to start)
- Content must reflect the brand voice — never commercial, always storytelling
- The public doll page is the primary conversion asset
- Universe announcements are the primary content type
- Content generated by digital production feeds into this strategy

## THE SYSTEM ARCHITECTURE

### Current tech stack
- Next.js 15 — main application (admin + public pages)
- Supabase — database and storage
- AI generation — Google Gemini and Anthropic Claude, switchable
- Standalone AI microservice — apps/ai-service
- Vitest — 254 passing tests
- Vercel — deployment

### Current architecture
The system has been refactored into a clean feature-based modular architecture:
- app/ — thin Next.js routes
- features/ — all business logic by domain
- lib/ — shared capabilities (AI, assets, pipeline, contracts)

### What is built
- Five-stage production pipeline with stage lock system
- Prompt injection system with brand guidelines
- Content management with persistence
- Public experience with full audio system
- Content variation engine
- Architectural refactor with protected APIs and service boundaries
- AI microservice with Google and Anthropic provider support

### What is not yet built
- Universe layer (data model, admin UI, universe management)
- Content access scope model (universe / character match / assigned / exclusive)
- Character brief extended fields in admin UI
- Story builder (template system, content builder, publishing lifecycle)
- Activity builder
- Universe discovery on public page (new arrivals, new content surfacing)
- Physical production tracking (Layer 2)
- Creative lab tools (Layer 1)
- Social media management layer
- Online shop
- CRM and order management
- Roles and permissions for collaborators

The system exists to serve one goal:
**transform a handmade doll into a child's most meaningful companion —
at the scale required to build a real business.**

The doll is the passport. The universe is the destination.
The universe grows. The passport never expires.
