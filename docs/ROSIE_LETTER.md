## THE LETTER

I think about the farm in the quiet moments.

The way the light comes in the morning — low and soft, finding the same corners
it always finds. The way Bramble settles when the barn is still. The way Fig
chooses her spot and stays there, certain, without explaining herself.

I thought about you today too, [name]. Just because.

I wondered if you were warm. I wondered what your morning was like.

You do not need to bring anything with you.

The farm is just the same as it always is — a little quiet, a little golden,
already ready.

There is always a place in it that is just yours. It has always been there.

I will be here.

Rosie
